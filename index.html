The Memory of Water

By Shelagh Stephenson

All Saints Lewes

The Memory of Water revolves around three sisters at the wake of their mothers funeral and is observes human nature and the emotional pendulum that swings from disharmonious chaos to sweet sublime family reunion. Although grounded in naturalism the direction and physical performance at times, verges on the surreal, bringing out the exquisite writing and the frequently unwitting comic lines.
